Formal Education

Structured, hierarchical, and institutionalized
learning that takes place in schools, colleges, or universities.

Informal Education

Unstructured, spontaneous learning acquired through daily life experiences, interactions with family, friends, media, and the environment.

Non-formal Education

Organized and systematic educational activity taking place outside the formal system, such as workshops, seminars, or community programs, focusing on specific skills.

Vocational Education

Practical-based training focused on acquiring specific skills, techniques, and knowledge required for particular trades or professions.

Distance Education

Learning that occurs when the teacher and student are physically separated, utilizing technology such as online platforms, correspondence, or digital media.

Special Education

Tailored instruction and support designed specifically to meet the unique needs and abilities of learners with disabilities.
